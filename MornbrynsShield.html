Mornbryn's Shield


General info

Small village on the edge of the Evermoors, built on a highpoint of a hill looking out over the moor. It has a keep/watchtower equipped with catapults

The population mostly consists of sheep herders and clothing makers, they sell mostly to larger towns like Triboar and Yartar


Campaign info

Party stayed here for the night in the Inn after escaping from the underdark, before their travel accross the Evermoor to Silverymoon

People/places of interest

The Moor's View: the only inn in town, most villagers gather here in the evening to drink and talk

General Goods store: decent supply of farming tools, very basic items and foods
