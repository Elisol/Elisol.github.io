Golden Oak


General info

Fifty miles south of Elventree, there is an oak grove populated with bats, most ordinary, with some azmyths and sinisters. An oak in the grove whose trunk diameter is exactly half the wingspan of a male Elven Court sinister is made of solid gold and disguised as a normal oak, it is thought to be a guard against Tomb Raiders and Robbers. Exactly which oak is not known.


Campaign info

People/places of interest

The depths of the forest of Cormanthor is where the family of Celephinnor had it's home. It's the place where he was born and where he was brought up according to the old traditions and values. At least, until his family was banished from the woods by the Elven council of Myth Drannor after the sudden death of his father and uncle. It was when his family left Cormanthor and travelled to friends of his grandfather in the High Forest that they were attacked by drow. Celephinnor and his grandfather were the only two that survived the attack.
