Everlake


General info

Small community of 9 farms on the Evermoor on the edge of the large lake

Campaign info

Party went here during their travel on the Evermoor to Silverymoon because they heard the town had been under attack several times by strange creatures. These turned out to be Duergar and a Quaggoth that were desperate for food. In a nearby cave they found a Duergar child

People/places of interest

Gilda: middle aged female gnome, opens up her home as a gathering place in the evenings and let's travellers sleep in the attic

Grun: Gilda's son, adept with a crossbow and desperate to protect the community

Lina and Elsie: mother and daughter sheepherders, mostly out on the moor with the herd

Bobby: goat farmer, sold the party a goat and wagon
